I am a fifth-year Ph.D. student in Decisions, Operations, and Technology Management at the Anderson School of Management, UCLA. My advisors are Auyon Siddiq of UCLA and Frederick Eberhardt of Caltech. My research is at the intersection of causal inference, economics, and operations. I am particularly interested in developing decision-making models that leverage large-scale data combining statistics, optimization, and machine learning.
